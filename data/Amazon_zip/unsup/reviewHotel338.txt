Only in your most desperate moments should you book a room here. I mean moments where, in order to survive the night, you should get rid of the gun and the hooker and get your ass to a meeting. Other acceptable circumstances include being a college student who's coming to scope out apartments in Chicago, and who wants to stay in a place that has locks on the doors and a private bath. In that case, one extra star for complimentary breakfast that's at least as good as the food on campus. Bonus: Prominently displayed certificate stating that it is a Most Improved Property. Yeesh.
