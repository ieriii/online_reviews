The Hatter Days Dinner at The Bad Apple was great! Thanks so much for all of the awesome food and beer last night. Everyone needs to go to the Bad Apple for the special food events! We had some of the best food and beer ever! The menu featured; Suckling pig hand carved by New Holland owner Fred Bueltmann, Pork Belly with brocolli rabe and cannellini beans, Hatter Battered fried oysters and pork crackling salad, Beets and pickled onions salad, Braised Lambs Tongue ravioli with smoked tomato and charred corn sauce, Caraway crusted venison with a parsnip puree, Goat Cheese cheescake and the best, Chi creme brulee!
