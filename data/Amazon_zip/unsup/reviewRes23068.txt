i remember being here a couple years ago (loved it!) and recently ordered carry out (and i'm still lovin it!). dolmades- you must order! stuffed grape leaves topped with egg lemon sauce. gyros- you get soooo much meat and their yogurt sauce is to die! mousaka- i'd like to think of this as a greek lasagna with eggplants and meat topped with the egg lemon sauce. the restaurant itself has a huge with a friendly waitstaff, loud and fun! there's valet and street parking (if you're lucky!).
