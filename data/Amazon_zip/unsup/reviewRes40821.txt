Long story short....I have been to many pho spots in Chicago and Tank has been by far the best. The food is fresh and delicious and the price is simply the cherry on the cupcake. It is always busy which is always reassuring, but it doesn't take long at all to get a table and be waited on by one of their super nice staff. I haven't had too many things off ohe menu but I haven't been disappointed once. This place gets an A
