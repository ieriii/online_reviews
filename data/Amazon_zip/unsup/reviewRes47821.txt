Similar to other Brazilian steak houses with an American twist. The cold food selection is good. The style is a bit different. They have about 11 meats for the night. 3 beef cuts, 2 pork, 2 chicken, 3 fish, 1 duck. The beef cuts are well prepared and not to salty like the other places. Excellent wine list. Dessert list is average. Approximately $50 per person without drinks, dessert, tip and tax.
