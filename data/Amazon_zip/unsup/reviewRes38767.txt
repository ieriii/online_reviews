Very attentive wait-staff. They deserve any tip you can share. Maybe it is because of my aging appearance. However, the food, the ambiance is spectacular. Mussels, fish, beef, tender and may I say juicy brisket done to perfection - all dishes overflowing with enormous mounds of frites (fries) which were more than perfectly served with aioli sauce which was,... right on dude. Bathrooms are a little dingy however and the patio is closed in the winter (bummer), but waiting 60 minutes is no big deal here... it is worth it with the excellent beer menu and friendly owner that makes sure everyone is happy.
