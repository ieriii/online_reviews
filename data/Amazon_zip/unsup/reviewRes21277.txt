Caldos much better than tortas, which are excessively bready. I'll stick to bowls which are excellent. And desserts are the real reason to come here. Churros will take years off your life but are probably worth it. They will also bring you drinks while you're waiting in line. We had some horchata that was excessively sweet but gave us the moral fortitude we needed to wait for 20 minutes after a day of walking around chicago.
