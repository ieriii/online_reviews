Carnivale is certainly a feast for the eyes as well as the Latin-lovin-tummy. It's obvious that no detail was overlooked in renovating what was probably a big warehouse into a sumptuous playground, with room after room that we only began to explore. So the food was good and I Loved my raspberry mojito, but for those of us with tighter-than-this-party-scene budgets, the menu prices will make you sigh a bit.  Perhaps rather than charging an admission to the circus, they simply tack on a few bucks here and there to menu items.  This place is worth visiting at least once. I can't afford such dining all too often, but I'll probably be back eventually. Dress Code: trendy, young, high credit card balances.
