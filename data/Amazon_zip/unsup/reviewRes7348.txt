Look here kids, this place is the single best date spot in the Chi. Now, I'm not saying it's the most romantic but it certainly is the best to grab a bite, dim the lights, and enjoy yourself with the opposite (or same if that's how you roll) sex. Bottom line: from the asparagus salad to the seafood risotto to the lamb to the chocolate rasberry cake I was thrilled. For the price point (lower upper) it was great.  Check it out and then go home and get your swerve on...
