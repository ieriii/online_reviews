I have never been here at night to drink so this review is simply for their breakfast / brunch / lunch. If you like bacon, this is the place for you. They don't skimp on any dish and it is in quite a few things. My lady and I went here for brunch for 6 consecutive weeks if that says anything. I tried something new every time and was happy every time. Bloody Mary - Tasty, corn rimmed glass, strip of bacon. Standard breakfast - Quite a a bit of food for $6; lots of bacon Sweet Potato Hashbrowns - AWESOME BLT - At least 1lb of bacon on this, delicious Eggs Benedict - GF swears by it Omelet - 5 egg omelet; gigantic
