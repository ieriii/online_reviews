What can I say about Mia Francesca, a celebration of flavors upon my tongue. We started off the meal with a wonderful beef carpaccio, the capers were the star of this dish. For an entre we shared the tortellini and the mahi mahi. I would go back again for the tortillini. To finish up the meal we were served cream puffs filled with pistachio ice cream and covered in a chocolate ganache..
