The food is delicious, unique and the seasonal specials keep things exciting. Sapori has nailed the romantic, candlelit vibe and is a great place to come for an intimate dinner. Be careful! This place will hit your wallet hard if don't keep an eye on the prices. Entrees average above $20 each, but promise large, filling portions. Be sure to sign up for their mailing list. They'll send you coupons for your birthday and keep you posted on other special offers!
