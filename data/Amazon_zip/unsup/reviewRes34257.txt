Sultan's will fix your hunger problem in a quick second and won't break your bank. And, it'll fill you up with really good, quality mediterranean food like smooth and almost milky hummus, freshly prepped falafel and really tasty pita bread. We've never had anything bad from this or its other location. Try the Jerusalem salad and make sure you ask for your falafel sanwdich to be prepared "spicy," For just about $5, your tummy will be purring with pleasure!
