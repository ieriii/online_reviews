The Art of Pizza is a spot that you should try. I am not a huge fan of the place but it's better than a lot of other pizzas in the neighborhood.  It is MUCH better than Pequods when you get it delivered. Pequods is HANDS DOWN the better pizza if you're eating at the restaurant itself. If I were you, I would not go out of my way to get a pizza from the Art of Pizza but if you're just looking a dependable delivery spot on the northside, then here you are...
