Was here with a huge group and did not have to wait in line for long = Yay! Waitress recommended we get the carafes by guaranteeing it would be there in 8 minutes -- it was actually 15 mins before we were served = Boo! When we saw the price of the carafes, we were also very shocked = Boo! Strawberry whiskey - Excellent Dark and Stormy - A bit too spicy on the ginger Gin and Home made tonic - Romeo and Juliet? - Excellent However, there is much to say about the ambience, the anonymity of the lounge. I was very impressed with the music selection and with the seating arrangement. Perhaps it will make more sense to order individual cocktails and come in a smaller crowd? Beware of long waits though... ps: They don't take reservations.
