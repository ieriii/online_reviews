Very few times does a place live up to it's hype.... And last. This place is LEGIT! No reservations, so you'll be tortured by a wonderful margarita or other tasty concoction these high quality bartenders whip up. Chips and salsa are as good as it gets. Into the dining room, attack anything with mole sauce. The busted dishes and carne asada is awesome! Three tips....enchiladas, enchiladas and enchiladas!
