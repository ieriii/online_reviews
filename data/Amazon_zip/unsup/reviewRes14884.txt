The Chateaubriand is unquestionably the best in the nation and easily goes toe-to-toe with France. Split it between three people and the portions remain huge while the check remains, well, in check. Cherries Jubilee is a can't miss-- oozy warm cherry goodness and delicious creamy vanilla ice cream in a perfect symphony of happiness. The regular bread would be an easy five stars if it weren't met by the pizza bread, which surely begs the question of six. Sauces are en pointe and the Key Lime martini tastes like good old fashioned pie, not the Dominick's stuff. Go here and you won't regret it.
