Unusual and totally fun! So, I was slightly uncomfortable using my hands to grab all the food and sweep it into my mouth however it's an experience you have to try if you like flavorful foods. If you've had Indian food and hate it, this might not be the right cuisine for you. Some of the entrees we ordered were spicy but not over the top. Everything we had was delicious and we ordered a variety of things including veggies and red meat, short ribs, ext... So, do the same and you won't be disappointed.
